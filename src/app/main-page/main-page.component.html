Welcome to SCM

We are a construction management agency that teams up with the owner of a project. We communicate to the contractor and architect on the owners behalf to aleviate the stresses of the construction world while educating owners during the construction process.

What We Do

  • Protect clients investments
  • Help clients understand the building process
  • Ensure clients vision is realized in planning stages of process/budgeting
  • Check behind the contractor in all stages of the project
  • Communicate between architects and contractor on behalf of client
  • Review change orders and find most cost effective solution
  • Provide best possible building/renovation experience

Why Choose Us?

We bring a small company, family type atmosphere to the big construction world making the owner feel as comfortable as possible during a process that can be very stressful without proper preparation and knowledge of working with major contractors.
